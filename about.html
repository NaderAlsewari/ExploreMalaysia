Welcome to Explore Malaysia!

Hi, I’m Nader Alsewari, and I'm deeply passionate about Malaysia’s history and culture. I created this blog to share my experiences and love for this incredible country. My goal is to provide food recommendations and cultural insights that will help visitors understand and appreciate Malaysia just like I do.

If you're planning your first trip or want to explore new places in this beautiful country, you're in the right place!

Why I Started Explore Malaysia

My love for Malaysia began when I first visited its diverse cities and natural wonders. I realized there was so much to discover, from the vibrant street food to the historical landmarks. That experience inspired me to create a platform where I could share my personal journeys and help others experience Malaysia's beauty.

Our Mission

Our mission is to showcase the incredible beauty and diversity of Malaysia. We aim to help travelers experience the rich culture, delicious food, and hidden gems of this beautiful country. Whether you're visiting for the first time or are a local looking to explore, we are here to inspire your next adventure.

Our Core Values

  • Authenticity - Real experiences.
  • Passion - Love for Malaysia.
  • Discovery - Exploring new stories.
